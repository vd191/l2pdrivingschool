Story Testimonial

L to P Driving School

Australian Capital Territory (ACT) accredited driving instructor.
Fluent in Vietnamese and English

For almost 20 years, I was the owner operator of a popular local Liquor Store. During this time, I have developed a variety of skills, I have trained people from different backgrounds and all ages.

I have often been the supervising driver for my family and friends as they learn to drive. With encouragement from them, I have pursued it as a profession. Feedback from them was always positive and they appreciated my way of teaching. The ease at which I communicate and my knowledge of road rules and safety has helped them to gain confidence to pass their test.

I became a qualified driving instructor in 2020, the rest is history. Being a driving instructor provides me with great work-life balance and the flexibility to be available to spend time with my family.

I really enjoy meeting new people, providing training and support. I am a calm, relaxed and approachable person.

I take great pride in guiding others to become safe and competent drivers, which in turn gives back to the community.
